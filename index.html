El Pollo Pelogroso - Help

Help Pepe Peligroso to find his way through the world of El Pollo Poligroso!

Collect coins to gain life and collect bottles for throwing them at enemies - but be careful: the endboss can only be beaten with bottles, so don't waste them!

Avoid collisions with enemies, even with the chickens. They may seem small but they can be painful!

Jump on them for beating them.

How to move Pepe?

← - Move left
→ - Move right
↑ - Jump
D - Throw bottle
